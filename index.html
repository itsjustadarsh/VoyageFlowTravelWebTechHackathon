üè∞ Jaipur

The Pink City

Royal Heritage of Rajasthan

Experience the grandeur of Jaipur with its magnificent palaces and forts. Visit the iconic Hawa Mahal, explore the majestic Amber Fort, and wander through the City Palace. The pink-hued buildings create a mesmerizing backdrop for your royal adventure.

Don't miss the astronomical wonders at Jantar Mantar, shop for traditional textiles and jewelry at Johari Bazaar, and savor authentic Rajasthani cuisine including dal baati churma and ghewar.

üèñÔ∏è Goa

Beach Paradise of India

Sun, Sand & Serenity

Relax on pristine beaches from Baga to Palolem, explore Portuguese heritage in Old Goa's churches, and experience the vibrant nightlife. Goa offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure with water sports, beach shacks, and stunning sunsets.

Visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus, cruise on the Mandovi River, trek to Dudhsagar Falls, and indulge in Goan seafood delicacies like fish curry rice and bebinca. The laid-back vibe makes it perfect for all types of travelers.

Beaches Adventure Nightlife

üå¥ Kerala

God's Own Country

Backwaters & Beyond

Cruise through the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey on a traditional houseboat, witness Kathakali performances, and rejuvenate with authentic Ayurvedic treatments. Kerala's lush landscapes include tea plantations in Munnar, wildlife in Periyar, and pristine beaches in Kovalam.

Experience the snake boat races, visit spice plantations, explore Fort Kochi's colonial charm, and savor traditional Kerala sadya on a banana leaf. The state's natural beauty and cultural richness create an unforgettable experience.

Nature Wellness Backwaters

üïâÔ∏è Varanasi

The Spiritual Capital

Ancient Mysticism on the Ganges

Witness the soul-stirring Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, take a sunrise boat ride on the holy Ganges, and explore ancient temples and ashrams. Varanasi, one of the world's oldest living cities, offers a profound spiritual experience that touches the heart.

Visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, explore the narrow lanes filled with silk weavers, attend classical music concerts, and experience the timeless rituals that have continued for thousands of years. Don't forget to try Banarasi paan and the famous street food.

Spiritual Heritage Culture

üåÜ Mumbai

The City of Dreams

Bollywood & Beyond

Experience the energy of India's financial and entertainment capital. Visit the iconic Gateway of India, explore the UNESCO World Heritage Victorian Gothic buildings, stroll along Marine Drive at sunset, and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of this never-sleeping metropolis.

Tour Bollywood studios, visit the Elephanta Caves, shop at Colaba Causeway, experience the buzz of Crawford Market, and indulge in Mumbai's famous street food - vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri. The city's cosmopolitan spirit welcomes everyone with open arms.
